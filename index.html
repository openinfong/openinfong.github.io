Nigeria's Premier Civic Database

Open Info NG centralizes Nigeria's essential civic information in one searchable hub. Explore verified data on institutions, public offices, services, laws, and much more. Designed to inform, empower, and mobilize, Open Info helps every Nigerian hold power accountable, navigate public services, and understand the systems built to serve them. Stay informed and take ownership of your civic space. Governance works better when citizens are informed and engaged.
