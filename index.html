Data Sources & Verification

About This Database

The Nigeria Government Contacts Database is a comprehensive directory of verified contact information for Nigerian government officials across all branches and levels of government. This resource is maintained through rigorous verification processes to ensure accuracy and reliability.

Data Sources by Category

  1. Senate Contacts: Sourced from the official National Assembly website (nass.gov.ng), Senate official directory publications, and verified through constituency offices.
  2. House of Representatives: Compiled from House of Representatives official directory, member constituency offices, and cross-referenced with official government publications.
  3. Police Commissioners: Data obtained from Nigeria Police Force official website (police.gov.ng), state command headquarters, and official police gazettes.
  4. Emergency Services: Information sourced from National Emergency Management Agency (NEMA), Federal Road Safety Corps (FRSC), and respective agency headquarters.
  5. Federal Ministers: Compiled from State House official publications, Federal Executive Council directory, and respective ministry headquarters.
  6. State Governors: Data verified through State Government websites, Government House official contacts, and state liaison offices in Abuja.

Verification Process

All contact information undergoes a multi-stage verification process:

  • Primary source verification from official government websites and publications
  • Cross-referencing with multiple independent sources
  • Regular quarterly updates to reflect changes in government positions
  • Direct verification through constituency offices where possible
  • Validation against official government gazettes and directories
Note: While we strive for maximum accuracy, government contact information may change between our quarterly updates. For critical communications, we recommend verifying contacts directly through official channels.
